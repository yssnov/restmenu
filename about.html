About Our Restaurant

Our Story

God loves the Trinity restaurant was established in 2018 with a simple mission: to create a place where people can come together to share exceptional food in a peaceful, welcoming atmosphere. Our name reflects our belief that dining is one of life's most sacred experiences.

We started as a small family business with big dreams. Our founders, Maria and Giuseppe Benedetto, immigrated from Italy with traditional recipes passed down through generations. They wanted to share not just their food, but their culture and values with the community.

Today, we continue to honor those traditions while embracing new culinary innovations.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Antonio Rossi

Chef Antonio brings over 15 years of culinary experience to God loves the Trinity. Trained in the finest kitchens of Rome and Florence, he specializes in combining traditional Italian techniques with modern presentation.

His passion for fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and attention to detail ensures that every dish that leaves our kitchen is prepared with love and precision.

Our Philosophy

At God loves the Trinity, we believe that food is more than just nourishment - it's a way to bring people together, create memories, and celebrate life's special moments.

We are committed to:

  • Using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients
  • Supporting local farmers and suppliers
  • Creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere for all our guests
  • Providing exceptional service that makes every visit memorable
  • Maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and safety
